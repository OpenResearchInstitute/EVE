Subject: Target of Opportunity request — Venus EVE radar reception at Effelsberg, October 2026

Dear Dr. Kraus,

Uwe Bach suggested I contact you directly regarding a target of opportunity
proposal for the Effelsberg 100-m telescope. Since the NorthStar portal is
currently offline for maintenance and the next regular deadline in September
falls after our observing window, Uwe recommended we approach you with a ToO
request.

The Open Research Institute (ORI) requests Effelsberg observing time during
the October 2026 Venus inferior conjunction to receive the bistatic radar
reflection from Venus transmitted by the Dwingeloo 25-m telescope (CAMRAS) at
1299.5 MHz, and optionally from additional transmitting stations at 2304 MHz.
This builds directly on the successful March 2025 Dwingeloo-Stockert EVE
experiment and extends it with the 100-m aperture Effelsberg provides.

Request summary:

  - Two L-band sessions: Dwingeloo transmitting at 1299.5 MHz, Effelsberg
    receiving with P217mm 7-Beam central horn. Requested dates approximately
    October 19-20 and October 25-26, 2026.

  - Two S-band sessions: one or more 2304 MHz transmitters (AMSAT-DL Bochum
    20-m is the primary candidate, with additional North American stations
    potentially participating), Effelsberg receiving with P170mm UBB.
    Requested dates approximately October 22-23 (inferior conjunction) and
    October 31 to November 1, 2026.

  - Total time request: 4 sessions × 2.5 hours + 0.5 hours overhead each = 12
    hours. Any two sessions constitute a scientifically complete result.

ORI handles all coordination with transmitting stations and data reduction.
Raw IQ data will be recorded in SigMF format and released publicly with no
proprietary period. MPIfR's operational role is telescope pointing, receiver
configuration, and IQ recording. No special operational support is required
beyond standard observing procedures.

Attached:

  - Technical justification including link budget (E_technical_justification.txt)
  - AI usage disclosure (E_ai_usage_disclosure.txt)
